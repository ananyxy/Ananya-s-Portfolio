Glossary on Jean Michel Basquiat

This glossary is a journey into the vibrant world of Jean-Michel Basquiat, one of the most iconic and influential artists of the 20th century. From his early days as a graffiti artist in New York City to his rise as a global art phenomenon, Basquiat's work is a fusion of raw emotion, cultural critique, and unbridled creativity.
