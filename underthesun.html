"Under the Sun"

In the heart of Kumbharwada, Dharavi, rows of pots bask in the warmth of the sun, their clay glowing with a golden hue. The air is thick with the scent of earth as these handcrafted vessels slowly dry, each one a testament to the skill and patience of the potters' craft. The sunlight dances on their smooth surfaces, casting shadows that tell stories of tradition and labor.

Shot on : Canon R50
